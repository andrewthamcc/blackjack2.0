Blackjack Rules

  • The attempt is to beat the dealer by having a count of 21, or as close to 21, without exceeding 21
  • Cards are valued their pip value
  • Face cards are valued at 10
  • Aces are valued at either 1 or 11
  • The dealer will always stand if they have a count of 17 or higher
  • If neither player or dealer has 21 whoever holds the higher hand wins
  • Either player or dealer exceeding a value of 21 loses the hand
